# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project aims to be a respectful, technically serious and collaborative space.

Participants are expected to:

- discuss ideas and implementations in good faith,
- provide actionable technical feedback,
- respect different experience levels,
- avoid harassment, personal attacks and dismissive behavior,
- keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- insults or hostile personal comments,
- harassment in public or private spaces,
- discriminatory language or behavior,
- deliberate disruption of technical discussions,
- publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit or reject comments, issues, pull requests or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately and include enough context to investigate the issue.
